# Spells

### Use `/spell` to display your current Spell. Spells will activate at their intercept rate only if you are attacking. You can only have one Spell at a time, but you can buy a replacement Spell, if you want to swap.

## Fireball

![Fireball](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/939309405227339776/1157377014257422346/fireball.png)

### Use `/buy spell fireball` and you can learn how to cast Fireball at the market.

Fireball Stats:

- intercept rate 20%
- attack 25
- bypasses armor

## Heal

## Feeblemind

![Feeblemind](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/939309405227339776/1157378723570536448/feeblemind.png)
