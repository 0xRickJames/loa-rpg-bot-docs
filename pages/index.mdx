# Lands of Ascension RPG Bot

**Lands of Ascension RPG Bot** is a Discord bot in [Lands of Ascension](https://lootheroes.io/)'s discord server, that users can interact with to buy items from a shop, battle each other, battle raid bosses and groups of raid bosses.

Only the most powerful can survive the relentless onslaught of FullMetaAlchemist!
